Whitehorse in Focus: A Visual Exploration

This project is all about capturing the beauty of Whitehorse through photos. We want to show off the city's natural wonders, urban scenes, cultural heritage, and community spirit. Through photography, we'll tell the story of Whitehorse and its people. From its natural wonders to its vibrant community, Whitehorse is a city full of beauty and life, waiting to be explored through a camera lens.
